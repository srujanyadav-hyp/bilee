Privacy Policy for Bilee

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Bilee ("we," "our," or "us"). Bilee is a paperless billing system that helps you manage digital receipts safely and efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Bilee, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Bilee, we collect:

  • Account Information:
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Display name
    • Profile photo (optional)
    • Password (encrypted)
  • Transaction Data:
    • Receipt information (merchant name, items purchased, prices, dates)
    • Payment methods used (UPI apps selected)
    • Transaction amounts and timestamps
    • Receipt photos (if you choose to attach them)
    • Manual expense entries
  • Budget & Expense Tracking:
    • Budget categories you create
    • Spending patterns and expense history
    • Financial analytics data

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information:
    • Device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • Unique device identifiers
    • App version
  • Usage Data:
    • Features used within the app
    • Session duration and frequency
    • Error logs and crash reports (for debugging)

1.3 Camera & QR Scanner

We access your device camera only when you:

  • Scan QR codes from merchant receipts
  • Attach receipt photos to manual expense entries

Camera access is requested only when needed and requires your explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services:
    • Create and manage your account
    • Store and organize your digital receipts
    • Generate expense reports and analytics
    • Track budgets and spending patterns
    • Enable receipt sharing and PDF generation
  • Improve User Experience:
    • Personalize your app experience
    • Optimize app performance
    • Fix bugs and technical issues
    • Develop new features
  • Security & Authentication:
    • Verify your identity
    • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
    • Secure your account and data
  • Communication:
    • Send transactional notifications (receipt confirmations, payment alerts)
    • Respond to your support requests
    • Provide important updates about the app

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages your login credentials securely
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your receipts, transactions, and profile data
  • Firebase Storage: Stores receipt photos (if uploaded)

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

3.2 Local Storage

Some data is cached locally on your device for:

  • Offline access to receipts
  • Faster app performance
  • Temporary session data

Local data is secured using:

  • Flutter Secure Storage (for sensitive data)
  • SQLite database (for receipts and transactions)
  • Encrypted SharedPreferences

4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We share your information only with:

4.1 Service Providers:

  • Google Firebase: Cloud infrastructure, authentication, and data storage
  • Google Cloud Functions: Backend processing for receipt generation

4.2 Third-Party Authentication:

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile information (name, email, profile photo)

4.3 Payment Apps (UPI):

When you initiate payments, we launch external UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.). We do NOT collect, store, or process your payment credentials or banking information. All payment processing happens directly in those apps.

4.4 Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulations.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

5.1 Access Your Data

  • View all your receipts, transactions, and profile information within the app

5.2 Update Your Data

  • Edit your profile information anytime
  • Modify or delete receipts and expenses

5.3 Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual receipts
  • Delete your entire account (which removes all associated data)
  • To request account deletion, contact us at: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]

5.4 Control Permissions

You can manage app permissions in your device settings:

  • Camera access (for QR scanning and photos)
  • Internet access (required for cloud sync)
  • Network state (to check connectivity)

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Receipts: Soft-deleted receipts are permanently removed after 30 days
  • Account Deletion: Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Children's Privacy

Bilee is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete it immediately.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules: Strict access controls ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed and encrypted
  • Regular Security Audits: We monitor for vulnerabilities and unauthorized access

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
